About the Project

Blogicum is a home for creative people. It is a community for people who see no boundary between blogging and friendship on social media.

Friendship and stories about new, unexplored experiences are what you will find on our platform. Millions of blogs on different topics: travel, politics, entertainment, fashion, literature, design, and every other sphere of human activity.

Creativity, diversity, and freedom of opinion and self-expression are the defining traits of our users.
